== Description ==

Click to mail - Wp Click To Mail Support is a WordPress plugin that allows website owners to easily add a Click to Mail bubble to their website. This Plugin enables website visitors to email you directly in 3 simple clicks to land on your direct consultation. As soon as they come to your website, and check your products or services in order to get those things they can mail you directly through the bubble button or the buttons we provided in the plugin. You will receive mail on your added email! You even can add Cbb & BCC email with custom email subject and body text. So mailing you will be easier than ever for your customers. Eventually, this plugin turns visitors into potential customers. The plugin also includes features such as bubble with a single agent with a pre-filled message, bubble with name and message form, customizable buttons, automatic message pre-fill, availability option based on your timezone.

== WHY Click To Mail Support ==

Emailing is one of the most popular way of communication in the modern world As a result, many businesses have begun using Mail as a customer support. The Click to Mail WordPress plugin has emerged as a popular solution for businesses looking to provide support to their customers.

First and foremost, The Click to Mail plugin is incredibly easy to install and use. It is a lightweight plugin that can be installed with just a few clicks. Once installed, you need to configure the bubble from settings panel to show chat bubble in your website as you want, it allows your customers to initiate a mail with you directly from your website. This convenience makes it easier for customers to get in touch with you, and in turn, can lead to higher customer satisfaction rates.

The plugin also allows you to customize the appearance and functionality of the Viber chat button. You can choose from various button styles, sizes, and positions to make it fit seamlessly with your website's design. Additionally, you can set up automated messages to greet your customers and provide them with helpful information or instructions.

In addition to providing instant support, The Click to Mail plugin allows businesses to save time and money. This, in turn, can reduce the workload of customer support teams, allowing them to focus on other important tasks.

Finally, The Click to Mail plugin allows businesses to build stronger relationships with their customers. By providing fast and efficient customer support, businesses can increase customer satisfaction rates and build a loyal customer base. This can ultimately lead to increased revenue and growth for the business.

The Click to Mail plugin is a popular solution for businesses looking to provide Viber-based customer support to your customers. Its ease of use, customization options, instant support capabilities, time and totally free of use, and customer relationship building features make it a powerful tool for businesses of all sizes.
